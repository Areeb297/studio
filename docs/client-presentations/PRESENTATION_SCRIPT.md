### Pain Point 1: Lost Paper Slips Causing Delays
**Current Problem:**
> "Right now, when a department needs to order supplies, they fill out a paper requisition slip. These slips get passed from person to person - from the department head to procurement, to the general manager for approval, then to accounting.
>
> But here's the problem: **paper slips get lost**. They sit on desks, get buried under other papers, or disappear entirely. This means delays in ordering, frustrated staff, and sometimes having to redo the entire request."

**Rahah24 Solution:**
> "With Rahah24, every purchase request is digital from day one. No more paper slips. Every request is tracked in the system with a unique number. You can see exactly where it is in the approval process at any moment.
>
> The system automatically notifies the next person in the approval chain. No more chasing people down. No more lost requests. Complete transparency and accountability."

---

### Pain Point 2: Low Stock Discovered After Purchase
**Current Problem:**
> "Here's a scenario that happens too often: The kitchen places an order for supplies. By the time the order is approved and arrives, you discover that you actually already had some of that item in stock - you just didn't know it.
>
> Or worse - the kitchen runs out of a critical ingredient during service hours because no one realized stock was low until it was too late.
>
> **This is a reactive approach** - you only discover problems when they happen. You're always one step behind, always firefighting instead of planning ahead."

**Rahah24 Solution - Proactive Stock Management:**
> "Rahah24 transforms your approach from **reactive to proactive** using AI-powered intelligent agents.
>
> **Real-Time Inventory Visibility:**
> Before anyone places an order, they can see exactly what's in stock right now across all locations.
>
> **AI-Powered Smart Alerts:**
> But here's where it gets intelligent - we're not just talking about simple low-stock alerts. Our AI agents continuously monitor your inventory and provide predictive insights:
>
> - **Predictive Alerts:** 'Based on your current consumption rate, you'll run out of rice in 3 days' - not when you're already out
> - **Seasonal Pattern Recognition:** 'Ramadan is approaching - historically you use 40% more flour during this period'
> - **Usage Anomaly Detection:** 'Oil consumption is 25% higher than normal this week - investigation recommended'
> - **Vendor Lead Time Optimization:** 'Order from Vendor A now to receive supplies before weekend rush'
> - **Expiry Warnings:** '15kg of chicken expires in 2 days - suggest using for today's special menu'
> - **Cross-Item Correlation:** 'You ordered tomatoes but typically need onions too - confirm order?'
>
> The system doesn't just tell you what's happening - it tells you what's **going to happen** and what you should do about it."

### Pain Point 3: Hours Spent Reconciling Purchase Orders
**Current Problem:**
> "At the end of each month, your accounting team spends hours - sometimes days - trying to reconcile purchase orders with actual deliveries and invoices.
>
> They're matching paper receipts, checking calculations, trying to figure out discrepancies. It's tedious, error-prone, and it delays your financial reporting."

**Rahah24 Solution:**
> "With Rahah24, reconciliation happens automatically. When a purchase order is created, it's linked to the delivery receipt and the invoice. The system automatically matches them up.
>
> If there's a discrepancy - say you ordered 100kg but only received 95kg - the system flags it immediately for investigation. What used to take days now takes minutes."

### Pain Point 4: General Manager Approval Delays
**Current Problem:**
> "Your General Manager is a busy person, managing multiple priorities. Purchase requisitions sit waiting for approval because:
> - They're in a meeting
> - They're traveling

> The system also supports **delegation** - if the GM is unavailable, they can delegate approval authority to their deputy temporarily. Operations never stop."
