## 📖 Executive Summary

**Rahah24 ERP** is an AI-powered, cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning platform specifically designed for Islamic educational institutions, restaurants, hotels, event management, and multi-business operations. Our mission is to provide **راحة (Rahah) - Comfort & Relief** through automated 24/7 accounting and operational management.

### **The Name - Rahah24**
- **Rahah** (راحة) = Comfort & Relief in Arabic
- **24** = Around the clock, 24/7 availability
- **Tagline**: *"24/7 Relief & Automated Hisab"*
