# **App Name**: Raha24 MVP

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: User authentication with role-based access (admin, staff).
- Recipe Costing: Recipe creation with ingredient costing, and automatic price calculation with markup.
- Daily Stock Management: Daily stock management for ingredients.
- Sales Recording: Record sales transactions manually.
- P&L Calculation: Calculate the daily Profit & Loss based on sales and stock data.
- AI-Powered Pricing Tool: Suggest optimal pricing based on recipe costs, analyze market rates, and suggest pricing based on similar meals.
- Demand Forecasting Tool: Use historic sales data to help predict demand patterns for menu items and make recommendations about stock needs.
- Events booking page: Weddings, conference, birthday and number of persons, date, venues available on that date which halls are available for the restaurant, rates, menu available, customer special requests, requirements, price quoted, discount available, advance payment, balance, specific terms and conditions.
- Financial ERP Layer: Balance sheet, trial balance, credits, debits, cash flows, expense vs profit breakdown, daily sales, cost, segment wise profitability analysis.
- Staffing & HR Integration: - AI tool suggests ideal staffing levels based on sales volume. - Maintain individual staff records. - Assign credentials and roles for login. - Optional features: biometric attendance, shift tracking.
- Customer Feedback Module: Customer Feedback Module
- Menu Management & Recipe Costing: Define which menu items are being sold. - Input standard recipes or import from kitchen staff. - Compare actual vs. standard recipes; flag deviations. - Lock agreed-upon recipes to ensure consistency. - Auto-calculate item-level menu prices based on: Ingredient cost Portion size & yield (e.g., 10kg biryani = 20 plates) Target gross profit multiplier (e.g., 2.25x or 2.5x)
- Department-Wise Cost Control: Link item issuance to departments (Pakistani, BBQ, Chinese, etc.) - Daily opening and closing stock tracking. - Monitor exact usage and consumption rates. - Track variances in real time.
- Sales, Profitability & Cost Analytics: Integration with POS for real-time sales monitoring. - Alternative: build a proprietary POS with credit card and terminal integration. Calculate: Food cost % Daily gross and net profit Expense contribution to net loss (utilities, salaries, etc.)
- Inventory Management Module: Build an in-house system replacing current software. Features include: Minimum, maximum, and reorder levels Vendor database (approved/open market) Controlled vendor registration with credentials Purchase history and item approval flow AI alerts for: Purchases above agreed rate Unusual spikes in consumption or wastage
- Customer Feedback Module: QR-code-based digital comment cards (via tablet or phone). - Forms synced with system (no manual Excel entries). Analyze: Most liked/disliked items Department-specific issues Staff/service-related complaints - Prevent staff manipulation of feedback.
- Marketing & Menu Intelligence: System reports: Fast items Slow-moving items Dead stock Suggests: Popular combinations Add-ons and up-sells Marketing recommendations (discounts, relaunches)
- Financial ERP Layer: Generate: Balance Sheet Trial Balance Expense vs. profit breakdown Monthly P&L statements Daily sales/cost/profit reports Segment-wise profitability analysis
- User Roles & Access: Multi-role system with unique logins: Owner Storekeeper Reception Floor staff (tablet order-taking or QR) Finance Marketing Email and messaging integration (WhatsApp / XChat). Role-based access control with real-time notifications.
- AI Based suggestions, analysis, charts, recommendations: AI Based suggestions, analysis, charts, recommendations

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Teal (#14B8A6) for a fresh, trustworthy, and modern feel, derived directly from the RAHAH24 logo.
- Background color: Very light gray (#F9FAFB), close in hue to the primary color, to maintain brand identity without overwhelming the UI.
- Accent color: Green (#10B981), an analogous hue to the primary color, to add emphasis on important elements like CTAs and success states.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, chosen for its modern and neutral appearance, which will promote clarity of the content; pairs well with technical applications.
- Code font: 'Source Code Pro' for displaying code snippets or technical data.
- Use simple, clean, and consistent icons throughout the application, following a modern and minimalist style.
- A clean and intuitive layout with a sidebar navigation for core modules.
